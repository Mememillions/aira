## Best Practices (from Docs)

1. **Always specify output schema** in prompts
2. **Include termination conditions** (max items, page limits, etc.)
3. **Handle edge cases** (CAPTCHA, login prompts, "Contact Us" pricing)
4. **Request structured errors** for debugging
